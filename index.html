Abstract

AccessEval provides the first large-scale benchmark to evaluate disability bias in LLMs, spanning six domains and nine disability categories. We benchmarked 21 LLMs and found systematic degradations in tone, stereotyping, and accuracy for disability-aware queries.

Overview of AccessEval Pipeline

Our proposed pipeline benchmarks disability bias by generating paired neutral and disability-aware queries across six domains and nine disability categories. Responses are evaluated on sentiment (VADER), social perception (Regard), and quality (LLM Judge).
